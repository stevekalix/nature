SAVE NATURE..

So, why do we need to save nature?

Because we depend on nature

We often forget, but humans are part of nature, not separate from it. Our survival depends on:

  • Clean air
  • Fresh water
  • Healthy soil for food
  • A stable climate

Without these, human life is at risk — not just plants and animals.

Because what we do to nature, comes back to us

  • Cut too many trees ➜ floods and droughts
  • Pollute rivers ➜ dirty water and diseases
  • Kill off animals ➜ imbalance in ecosystems
    It’s a cycle. What we give, we get back.

Because nature teaches us balance

Nature is the best example ofbalance and harmony. It inspires:

  • Better design (biomimicry)
  • Healthier lifestyles
  • More respect for life in all forms

Topic: The Importance of Saving Nature

  • We have the power to protect or destroy. If we can help, then we should. It’s about being responsible humans.

“No nature, no future”
